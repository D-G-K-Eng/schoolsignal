Sidekick

Your kid's school, handled.

The Problem

Parents today receive school communications across a fragmented mess of channels — email, Google Classroom, school apps, newsletters, automated dialers. Nothing is prioritized. There's no single place to see what actually requires action. Important permission slips, homework deadlines, and event signups get buried under noise.

Making it worse: platforms like Google Classroom don't allow API access. Schools can't push structured data out. Everything ends up as an email or a forward — which means the only practical way to catch it all is to monitor the parent's inbox.

"I missed Grayson's field trip permission slip because it came in buried under three newsletters on a Tuesday morning. It was urgent. It looked like everything else."

What Sidekick Does

Sidekick monitors a parent's inbox, reads every school email, and uses AI to separate signal from noise. Urgent items surface immediately. Everything is classified, dated, and tied to the right child. The parent sees only what matters — and gets notified before it's too late.

🔍
Smart Classification AI reads every school email and sorts it: action required, reminder, FYI, or noise. No more scanning everything yourself.
📅
Calendar Integration with Homework Links Due dates extracted from emails and added directly to your calendar — with the original email and any homework links attached to the event.
🔔
Urgent Alerts Permission slips, payments, and RSVPs trigger an immediate push notification. You don't wait for a digest.
📬
Gmail Forward Support Works with forwarded email — Grayson's Google Classroom notifications forward to Doug's inbox and get caught automatically.
👶
Multi-Child Tag signals by child. Filter your feed. Never mix up which kid has what due.
☀️
Morning Digest One clean summary every morning: what's due today, what's coming up, what needs your signature.
📖
Tutor Brief Auto-generated brief sent to your child's tutor before each session — assigned homework, upcoming tests, and a feedback loop back to you. No login required for the tutor.
